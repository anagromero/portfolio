AquaVenture

The game was created as a component of the Game Development course within the Master's Degree program at Ilmenau University of Technology. AquaVenture is a spellbinding and enigmatic game prototype that invites you to embark on a thrilling underwater adventure. The game's name is a clever combination of "Aqua", which represents the watery depths, and "Adventure", encapsulating the essence of exploration and discovery that awaits you. In AquaVenture, you assume the role of a skilled submarine pilot, navigating the uncharted oceanic abyss in search of valuable gems along the route and uncovering the mysteries of a captivating undersea cavern. Throughout this expedition, you must evade aquatic creatures seeking to impede your progress. In the end, AquaVenture is a game of skill, strategy, and exploration, where careful navigation and quick thinking are the keys to success.

The creation of this project involved a team of five students from the same course, with each member assuming a specific role:

Project characteristics:
  • Title: AquaVenture

  • Duration: October 2021 - February 2022
  • Prices: 3rd place at the Student Game Developer Conference 2022 - Certificate
  • Project partners: TU Ilmenau & Erfurt University of Applied Sciences

Implementation

 Development:
  • Genre: Endless Runner (3D Action / Adventure)
  • Platform: PC
  • Input
    • Keyboard: Arrow & WASD keys
    • Smartphone
      • Scanning of QR Code via Firefox
      • WebSocket connection to Web Server
    • Controller (Controller/ D-Pad)
  • Gameplay:
    • Submarine escaping from a huge shark
    • Collection of gems: option to "buy" other submarine models
    • Difficulty strategy: speed increase over time
    • Game Over: Any collision against the walls of the caves or some of the creatures
  • World generation: Random chain generation of predefined rooms/tunnel
